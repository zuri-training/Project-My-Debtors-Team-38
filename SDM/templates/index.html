What is "Student Debt Monitor"?

This is a web application designed specifically for school owners and administrative heads of Primary and Secondary schools in mainland, Lagos, Nigeria to enable them to see the debt status of students before granting them admision into their schools.It also acts as a bridge between schools and debtors as it lets them monitor their debt status.
